# Studio Taal - Bollywood Dance Programs for Kids

## About Studio Taal

**Mission**: Bollywood dance programs for children ages 3–14. Our mission is to nurture each child's unique potential while celebrating Indian culture.

**Belief**: "Every child has a dancer within them."

## Programs Offered

### Junior Rock Stars (Ages 3–7)
Fun introduction to Bollywood dance featuring:
- Simple moves and choreography
- Colorful props and costumes
- Focus on coordination, confidence, and creativity

### Young Performers (Ages 7–10)
Advanced training including:
- Advanced choreography
- Fusion styles
- Stage performance preparation
- Technique building and cultural appreciation

### Teen Performers (Ages 11–14)
Professional-level training featuring:
- Choreography creation
- Leadership development
- Cultural ambassadorship
- Professional performance skills

**All programs include:**
- 30-minute classes
- Stage performance opportunities
- Small group settings
- Video tutorials for practice
