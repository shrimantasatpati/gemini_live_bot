# Oscar's Landscaping Company

## About Oscar's Landscaping

Oscar's Landscaping Company provides comprehensive outdoor solutions for residential properties in the greater Seattle area. Our team of skilled professionals brings decades of combined experience and state-of-the-art equipment to every project.

## Services Offered

### Lawn Renovation (Spring & Fall)
Complete lawn rejuvenation services:
- **Dethatching**: Removing dead grass layers for better absorption
- **Aerating**: Creating small holes for air, water, and nutrients
- **Topsoil Application**: Adding nutrient-rich soil
- **Fertilizer Application**: Promoting healthy grass growth
- **Moss Killer Application**: Controlling moss growth
- **Overseeding**: Increasing lawn density

### Regular Maintenance (Weekly/Bi-weekly, No Contracts)
- Mowing, trimming, edging
- Weeding beds
- Blowing debris
- Shrub trimming
- Minor pruning
- Moss control
- Seasonal fertilization
- Fall leaf cleanup

### Special Projects & Enhancements
- Bark/mulch application
- SOD installation (instant lawns)
- Pavers/porcelain patios
- Retaining walls
- Sprinkler systems
- Ponds and fountains
- Fencing and wood staining
- Tree removal and planting
- Custom drip systems
- Hedge trimming and pruning
- Seal coating
- Soft washing (low-pressure cleaning)
