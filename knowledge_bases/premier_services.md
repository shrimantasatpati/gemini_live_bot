## Core Services

### Lawn Renovation (Spring & Fall)
**Premium Lawn Renovation Package - Starting at $129**

Includes:
- **Dethatching**: Removal of dead grass tissue (thatch) for better absorption
- **Aerating**: Making small holes for air, water, and nutrient penetration
- **Topsoil Application**: Adding nutrient-rich soil for fertility
- **Overseeding**: Spreading new grass seeds for density
- **Fertilizer Application**: Nutrients for healthy grass growth
- **Moss Killer Application**: Treatment to eliminate moss growth
